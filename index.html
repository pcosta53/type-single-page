HONK

VARIANT FONT

History

Honk is a variable color font created by Ek Type, a type foundry originating in India. The design of Honk was collaborated by Noopur Datye and Yesha Goshar, and engineered by Sidharth Jaishankar and Athul Jayaraman. The font is bold in shape and color. There are two axis to the font. You will see this font displayed on Indian trucks. Honk is a modular system with ten distinct styles, shadows, and color pallettes.

The font has two Axis, shadow and morph. Both variations are in the full value below.
